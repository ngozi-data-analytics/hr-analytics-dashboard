# HR Analytics Dashboard – Workforce Overview

## Project Overview
This project presents an HR analytics dashboard developed using Power BI to analyze workforce distribution, salary allocation, employee ratings, and gender composition across departments.

---

## Business Problem
HR leaders require a clear view of workforce structure, compensation, and performance to support workforce planning and decision-making. This dashboard answers questions such as:
- How is staff distributed across departments?
- What is the total salary cost?
- How does workforce composition vary by gender?
- Which employees and departments drive salary spend?

---

## Dataset
- Cleaned HR employee dataset
- Time period: 2016–2018
- Key fields: Department, Gender, Salary, Performance Rating, Year

---

## Tools Used
- **Power BI** – Data cleaning & transformation, DAX, interactive dashboards

---

## Key Metrics
- **Total Headcount:** 100
- **Total Salary:** $8M
- Staff by Department
- Staff by Gender
- Salary & Performance Ratings by Employee

---

## Key Insights
- Procurement and Website departments recorded the highest staff count.
- Salary distribution shows concentration among a small group of high-earning employees.
- Gender distribution is relatively balanced across the organization.
- Performance ratings vary across departments and roles.

---

## Business Impact
This dashboard supports HR teams by providing visibility into workforce structure, compensation trends, and employee performance, enabling data-driven HR decisions.
